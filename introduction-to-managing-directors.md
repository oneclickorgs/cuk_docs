* In the **Directors** section, the Secretary can make sure the register of Directors and Officers is up-to-date.
* Directors can be appointed from the Members, or an external director can be appointed.
* Officers must be appointed from the current Directors.
* If you have an AGM with an online election of Directors, the system will automatically retire and appoint Directors as necessary based on the results of the election.
* In all other cases, you will need to manually update the register of Directors and Officers.
